# Delivery Reflection / 交付复盘

## What this project taught me / 这个项目带来的经验

- Field projects require algorithm design and hardware constraints to be considered together / 野外项目必须同时考虑算法设计和硬件约束
- Robustness is more important than ideal accuracy / 鲁棒性通常比理想精度更重要
- Data continuity and clock synchronization are often more critical than the positioning solver itself / 数据连续性和校时往往比定位求解器本身更关键
- A good field solution must include failure recovery and manual verification mechanisms / 好的现场方案必须包含故障恢复和人工验证机制
