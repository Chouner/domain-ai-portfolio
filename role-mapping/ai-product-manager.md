## Example Project Description

### Acoustic QA Agent

Problem: Users had difficulty understanding sonar products and basic acoustic concepts.

Product decision: Build a knowledge-base QA Agent connected to the official WeChat account.

Metrics: 100 core terms, 3789 knowledge descriptions, 6-month trial, 218 users.

Limitation: Pricing and customized engineering scenarios still require human handoff.

中文版本：为声呐产品教育和基础声学问答设计 AI 客服流程。核心指标包括 100 个术语、3789 条知识描述、6 个月试运行和 218 位用户；价格和定制化工程场景仍需人工转接。
