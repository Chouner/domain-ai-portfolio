## 4. Portfolio Logic / 作品集逻辑

The projects are not arranged as a timeline. They are arranged as a capability portfolio: domain problem framing, AI workflow design, algorithm engineering, multimodal data processing, evaluation design, and delivery-oriented documentation.

这些项目不是按时间线堆叠，而是按能力组合组织：专业问题定义、AI 工作流设计、算法工程化、多模态数据处理、评估设计和交付型文档。
