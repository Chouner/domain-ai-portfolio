# Domain AI Project Lifecycle

## 1. Task Source
Customer requirement, expert pain point, data bottleneck, product opportunity, technical reserve, research exploration.

## 2. Problem Framing
Who has the problem, current workflow, bottleneck, why AI is needed, and what simple automation cannot solve.

## 3. Domain Constraints
Physical constraints, data quality constraints, sensor constraints, operational constraints, expert knowledge constraints.

## 4. Solution Design
Rule-based method, algorithm module, RAG, Agent workflow, multimodal model, hybrid AI + traditional algorithm.

## 5. MVP Validation
Minimal dataset, workflow, interface, and evaluation metric.

## 6. Evaluation
Accuracy, recall, stability, intervention rate, traceability, latency, cost.

## 7. Delivery
Documentation, demo, API/interface, test report, and handover checklist.

## 8. Iteration
Failure cases, missing data, model limitations, next version.
